Sarojini Naidu

" The Nightingale of India , or 'Bharat Kokila"


Bharat kokila

Time-line of her Life..

  • 1879Born in Brahmangaon Bikrampur, Dhaka, Bengal
  • 1895 to 1898 she studied in England, at King's College, London and then Girton College, Cambridge
  • 1898 Returned to Hyderabad and same year she married Govindaraju Naidu, a physician.
  • 1904Naidu became an increasingly popular orator, promoting Indian independence and women's rights, especially women's education.
  • 1906Joined in National Congress party
  • 1907 She was the second woman President of the Indian National Congress and first indian woman to preside over the INC conference .
  • 1917established the Women's Indian Association.
  • 1928Awarded Hind Kesari medal.
  • 1949Died on March 2, 1949, Lucknow by heart attack.
